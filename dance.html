So You Think Ants Can Dance!

In this project I took mocap data and used it to perform different types of animations on the "ants". This included creating a looping animation that smoothly interpolates between the beginning of the motion clip and the end to avoid any discontinuities. Additionally the ants would perform a given dance action based on a button press and overlaid new motion clips onto the ants at runtime. I composed transformations in a hierarchy to create a scene graph and an animated character within a scene.
